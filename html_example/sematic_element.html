About Our Department

The Department of Computer Engineering at Khon Kaen University is a leading institution in northeastern Thailand, dedicated to excellence in education, research, and innovation in computer engineering and technology.

Our department was established in 1987 and has grown to become one of the most respected computer engineering programs in Thailand. We offer comprehensive education from undergraduate to doctoral levels.

Academic Programs

We offer the following degree programs:

  1. Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Engineering
    4-year undergraduate program focusing on hardware and software integration
  2. Master of Engineering in Computer Engineering
    Advanced graduate studies with research focus
  3. Doctor of Philosophy in Computer Engineering
    Research-intensive doctoral program for academic and industry leaders
